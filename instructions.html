Find The Bone: Instructions

Welcome to the instructions page! Follow these steps to complete the task:

  1. The player is a dog who is roaming around the house backyard.
  2. In the backyard there is a bone burried underground (The Treasure).
  3. You can change your position by a single click on your desired location.
  4. Assuming that you are currently at the location where you last clicked, you have to read the corresponding clue and crack where is the bone is buried.
  5. Remember: The clues are the thoughts that crosses your mind by the smell at a particular point in the backyard.
  6. Try to find the bone in minimum number of clicks to win!
